Our Fundamentals

It might seem crazy to learn computer science without computers, but sometimes the craziest ideas make the most sense. Learning offline means more hands-on interaction and a better grasp on fundamental concepts without getting tangled in complicated syntax. Our curriculum is also designed to give students without access to tech an opportunity to learn—hip hip hooray for inclusivity! Now take your computational thinking skills to the next level by exploring our exciting planets.

Our Planet Breakdown

Our curriculum is based on a galaxy of planets that represent the six core concepts of computer science: decomposition, algorithms, abstraction, logic, patterns, and evaluation.
