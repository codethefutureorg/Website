About Us

Meet the team!

Our Story

We started with a mission to make computer science education an inclusive subject for all students, not just those with access to technology. After months of brainstorming and inventing, we built our CS Fundamentals curriculum to teach computer science in an engaging, yet technology-free way. At Code the Future, we're all about inclusivity!
